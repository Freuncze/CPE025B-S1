Let's cook Spaghetti Alla Amatriciana

Serves 6

Referenced from Recipe Wiki

Ingredients:

  • Olive oil: 2 tbsp
  • Large onion: 1, thinly sliced
  • Garlic: 2 cloves, crushed
  • Bacon: 6 lean slices, diced
  • Dry white wine: 1/4 cup
  • Peeled tomatoes: 1 lb, drained
  • Salt: 1/2 tsp
  • Pepper: 1 tsp
  • Dried oregano: 1/2 tsp
  • Spaghetti: 1 lb, cooked for 10-12 minutes, drained, kept hot
  • Parmesan cheese: 1 cup

Preparation:

  1. In pan, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  2. When oil is hot, add onion and garlic.
  3. Fry, stirring occasionally for 5 to 7 minutes or until onion is soft but not brown.
  4. Stir in the bacon and cook for another 4 minutes.
  5. Add wine and bring liquid to boil.
  6. Boil rapidly for 2 minutes, then add the tomatoes.
  7. Stir in the salt, pepper and oregano and bring liquid to boil again.
  8. Reduce heat to medium low and continue cooking for 15 minutes more.
  9. Stir occasionally.
  10. Remove from heat.
  11. Put spaghetti in large deep serving dish and pour over the sauce, using 2 large spoons.
  12. Toss mixture until thoroughly coated with sauce.
  13. Sprinkle over the grated cheese and serve at once.
